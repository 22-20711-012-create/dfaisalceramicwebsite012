THE ARTIST

I am a ceramic artist who explores clay through emotion, texture, and natural form. My work focuses on imperfection, simplicity, and handmade expression.

Philosophy

Clay is not forced, it is guided. Every shape carries movement, emotion, and time. I believe ceramics should feel alive, not perfect. Each piece becomes its own identity.

Artistic Styles

Minimal Clay Form

Hand-Thrown Pottery

Organic Texture

Modern Ceramic Art
